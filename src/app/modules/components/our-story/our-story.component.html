Our Story

In October 2021, Anju K (CEO & Founder) realized the reason behind her survival during one domestic violence awareness event at Our Lady of Lake University. The reason was - to share her story, to fight for herself as only she knew what happened, other than her abuser.

(Domestic Violence Happens Behind the Closed Doors)
At the event, she came to know about the existed & available resources, not everyone knows all of them. If community knows about them, they really don’t trust them or reach out to them.

She came up with an idea to write her experiences / testimonials for the resources which she found helpful for herself. When she started working on it, she was a resident of BWCS, and some kindhearted woman gave her $20 to take her kids to have burgers and fries. Instead, with the support of her kids, she registered the domain Healing2Peace.com in November 2021.

As her medical condition was not aligned with her purpose, she couldn’t start working on the website. Then her 10 years old son designed and created the website. Soon the mom-son duo launched the blog website on January 1, 2022.

Within 6 months, Anju K reached 50+k people around the globe through her blogs and vulnerability, the numbers were remarkable.

She started getting featured in magazines, podcasts, and local news. By now Healing2Peace became a community, a safe space for others too. She started connecting and helping others from different countries and time zones. There came a time when she was working 16-18 hours, which declined her health even more, she needed to organize everything, and this was her healing too, so pauses to relax were essential.

She didn’t have a team or money to keep going, even then she started application for Healing2Peace to get registered. She was new to the legal system and lacked the documents needed. And one day, a blessing came to her, she found an attorney who offered her help in the process. With the help of her attorney friend, she took a flight of her dreams and took made it Healing2Peace, LLC.

Now she was an expert to survive almost every struggle, she took the road less travelled or never travelled and decided to serve the community, made a business from the survivors, for the survivors.

At present, we provide different healing & educational programs by focusing the individual needs & situations. We raise awareness and build relationships between available resources and the community. We are here to help you heal and grow in the right direction.

To know more how we can support you, check out the services, shops, resources and connect with us!
