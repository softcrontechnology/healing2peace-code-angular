Group Coaching

Sharing & learning from each other’s experiences is much needed to heal and grow personally/professionally. When you share the stories of your victories and defeats, healing starts unfolding naturally and you come out as a new person who is more resilient, calm and wise. By sharing your stories, you stay in touch with your true self and process your past in an effective way. It helps you along with your listeners. You build a community of trust and vulnerability. It boosts your self-esteem and confidence.

These groups are hosted by our CEO, Anju K and other professionals like counselors, speakers, and survivors like you. You are a boss here; you have the power to share your story as you know your story better. We are here to listen you, support you without any judgements. These are conducted once a week, we start new group every month. You can join as much as you want according to your availability and healing journey. You don't need to suffer, help is available.
