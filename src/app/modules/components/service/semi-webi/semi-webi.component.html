Webinars & Seminars

Time-to-time, we conduct in-person seminars and virtual webinars on different topics for personal and professional growth. Our programs are specially designed for domestic violence and abuse survivors as we understand their special needs and concerns. We know how trauma can affect a person’s life, but it doesn’t define them in any way. It was not your trauma that made you strong, it was the way you handled it and now you want to be the best version of yourself.

We are planning to organise seminars quarterly or half-yearly depending upon the number of attendees. For webinars you can join them every month virtually as per your convinience. During the webinars we give presentation, discuss different topics which are associated and helpful in your healing. Keep coming back or subscribe us to keep yourself updated about the services and the upcoming events. Also, you can follow us on different social media platforms to stay connected .
