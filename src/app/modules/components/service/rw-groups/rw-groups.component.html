Reading/Writing Groups

Reading helps you to understand the world around you & writing helps you to understand yourself in better ways. Reading the right content at the right time increases productivity and expressive writing reduces stress, anxiety, and depression. With the combination of both, you can find focus and clarity about your passion and purpose. This also helps in organizing your thoughts and give a meaning to your life. Reading good content is food to your mind just like eating is to your body.

These groups are focused on your healing via both techniques and conducted by professionals and published authors. By joining these groups you feel motivated and get clarity about you goals in your life. This is a series of different sessions stretched over 2 months but can be changed according to the individual requirements. These are from our signature module model but focused on trauma surviours as their needs are different.

Also if you want to write & publish your own writings, we are here to help you in that too. You never know when you get to become a bestseller!
