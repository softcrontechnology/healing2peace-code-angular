Re-Gain Workshops

Art & Creativity helps in emotional healing. After coming out of any trauma, a person goes from different phases of pain, grief, or loss. Most DV and abuse survivors lose themselves somewhere in the piles of their past. They need to release that collection of unhealed traumas in a healthy way to heal and restart their lives. But as nothing stops the life, it becomes difficult for them to take a pause and work on their inner child. Art & Creativity helps in decreasing stress and perception of pain, it imporves the quality of life. It is prooved that 45 minutes of creative activity can make a huge difference in mental health.

With our Re-Gain workshops, we help you to forge a connection between your mind, body, and soul. These workshops are designed & hosted by our founder, Anju K herself. She works on personally to get to know what works for you best like art, craft, writing or creating something to heal you from deep inside. We specialize in using the leftover, thrifted or rejected pieces to create something new which is beautiful, meaningful and sustainable just like you. We help you in getting a stability with your skills & interests. We support you in a customized way so that you become emotionally and financially stable.

Also, this helps to remember that you were worthy, and you are still worthy. Only you need to REDISCOVER yourself!
