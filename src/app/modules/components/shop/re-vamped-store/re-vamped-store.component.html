Re-Vamped Store

Let’s do our part-Recycle, Reuse and support your community!

Most of the time, survivors have things they can’t keep because of the their financial needs, moving out, or can’t find a safe place to do any garage sale, we help them in selling their things and clothes. We take care of the handling and sales, which helps them to start a new life with some money. All the products are in good condition/slightly used and looking for a new consumer.

Start Shopping

When a survivor starts a new life, they have to start from zero without much financial support. With our RE-Vamped Store, we help them to find their necessities at an affordable price. If you have anything, you are planning to get rid of, please contact us, maybe it finds a new consumer.
